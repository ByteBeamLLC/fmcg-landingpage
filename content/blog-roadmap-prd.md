## SEO Guidelines

### On-Page Optimization Checklist (Apply to Every Blog)

#### Title Tag
- Maximum 60 characters
- Primary keyword near the front
- Include year for freshness (2025/2026)
- Include location for GCC content

#### Meta Description
- Maximum 155 characters
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Compelling call-to-action
- Include value proposition

#### Header Hierarchy
```
H1 (1 per page) - Primary keyword
  └── H2 - Major sections (include secondary keywords)
      └── H3 - Subsections
          └── H4 - Details (if needed)
```

#### Content Optimization
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Keyword density: 1-2% (natural, not stuffed)
- Related keywords integrated naturally
- Minimum 2,000 words for pillar content
- 1,200-1,500 words for supporting content

#### Internal Linking Requirements
- Link to relevant ByteBeam product pages
- Link to related blog posts (when published)
- Link to industry landing pages
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

#### External Linking
- 2-3 authoritative sources per blog
- Link to government/regulatory bodies for compliance content
- Link to industry reports and statistics

#### Image Optimization
- Descriptive file names (e.g., `uae-food-label-requirements-2025.png`)
- Alt text with keyword variation
- Compressed for web (WebP format preferred)
- At least 2-3 images per blog

## Style Guide

### Voice and Tone
- **Professional but accessible**: Write for smart business people, not developers
- **Practical over theoretical**: Focus on actionable guidance
- **Confident but not arrogant**: Position as helpful expert
- **Clear and concise**: Avoid jargon; explain terms when used

### Writing Standards
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences under 25 words on average
- Use bullet points for lists (3+ items)
- Include data and statistics with sources
- Break up long sections with subheadings

### ByteBeam Mentions
- One mention in first third of article (brief, relevant)
- One detailed section near end (solutions section)
- Never more than 15% of word count about ByteBeam
- Always tie to reader benefit, not feature list

### Call-to-Action Guidelines
- One primary CTA at end (demo or trial)
- One lead magnet CTA (download)
- Optional: In-line CTA if highly relevant
- Never more than 3 CTAs per article

### Image Guidelines
- At least 2-3 images per blog post
- One infographic/diagram recommended
- At least one ByteBeam screenshot
- Alt text with keyword variation
- Compressed for web performance
